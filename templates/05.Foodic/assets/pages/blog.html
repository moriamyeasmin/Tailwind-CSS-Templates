Perfect Homemade Pasta Recipe

Herb-Crusted Salmon Recipe

From Epicurean Delights - www.epicureandelights.com

Ingredients

  • • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • • 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • • 1 lemon (zest and juice)
  • • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix parsley, dill, lemon zest, garlic, and breadcrumbs.
  3. Season salmon with salt and pepper. Brush with olive oil.
  4. Press herb mixture onto salmon fillets.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until salmon flakes easily.
  6. Drizzle with lemon juice before serving.
